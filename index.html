Brook Trout Heaven

A lifetime devoted to wild brook trout and northern waters

Brook Trout Heaven is a personal and educational website dedicated to wild brook trout and the cold, clean waters they depend on. This site reflects decades spent observing northern rivers and lakes in Northwestern Ontario.

Brook trout are a measure of water quality and intact habitat. This site exists to document their world and encourage respect for fragile cold-water ecosystems.
